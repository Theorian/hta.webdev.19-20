You run into an elderly woman in the park late one night. You consider mugging her, until you see that one of the cats she was petting before you walked over is now brushing against your leg. "They're quite pretty, aren't they?" The woman coos. Putting away your knife, you gently pick up the one currently rubbing against you. Up close, you notice the cat is made up of shadows, and has four eyes. "I think they'd like to help you, if you give them the chance."
